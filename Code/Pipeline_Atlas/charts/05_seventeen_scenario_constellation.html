Chart 05 · Scenario Sweep

Seventeen scenarios as a constellation.

Every scenario in the sweep is a star in 3D parameter space: omega on x (neighborhood weight), alpha on y (inertia toward existing class), rate on z (annual growth rate). Star size = 2030 built-up area; color = scenario family. Hover any star for its full parameter signature; the visual lets you see at a glance how the 17 scenarios cluster in design space.

Parameter ranges

Family clusters
3 + 6 Cyan/green/red stars trace straight lines along their parameter axis. Gold "hand-tuned" stars sit off-axis where multiple parameters move together.
Boom vs Conservation
extreme corners The brightest gold pair: boom (high rate, low alpha, low omega) and conservation (low rate, high alpha, high omega) sit at opposite corners of the parameter cube.
