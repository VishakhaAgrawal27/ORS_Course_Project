Chart 01 · Time & Cadence

Sixty years of built-up area, drawn as a clock spiral.

Each glowing node is a five-year mark in the city's growth. Nodes orbit on a vertical helix; cyan nodes are historical (1990 to 2020), red nodes are predicted (2025 to 2050). Node radius from the central axis = built-up area in km2. The visual tightening from 2030 onward signals the +12.0% per-period growth the model finds.
