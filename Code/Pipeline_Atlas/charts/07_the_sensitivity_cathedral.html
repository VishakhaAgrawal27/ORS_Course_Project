Chart 07 · Scenario Sweep

The cathedral of compactness.

Three columns of 3D bars, one per parameter family. Bar height = the 2030 compactness this scenario produces. Hover any bar for its parameter value and exact compactness. The visual punchline is the height difference: the rate column is roughly 13.65× taller than the omega column and 52.83× taller than the alpha column. The model's behavior is dominated by how fast you tell it to grow, not by the spatial weighting of where.
