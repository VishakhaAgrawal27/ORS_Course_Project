The new built-up — colored by what it used to be.

A full 3D extrusion of Hyderabad's predicted 2050 footprint. Pixels that were already built in 2020 rise as the dark core; pixels the model adds by 2050 rise shorter and are colored by what they replaced. Green = vegetation eaten, ochre = barren consumed, blue = water (rare). Toggle filters at bottom to isolate one origin at a time.
