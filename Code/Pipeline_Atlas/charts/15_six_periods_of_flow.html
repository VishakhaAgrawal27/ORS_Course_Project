Chart 15 · Spatial Reveals

Six five-year flows, stacked through time.

Each translucent slab is one of the six prediction periods (2020 to 2025, 2025 to 2030, etc.) viewed as a layered Sankey. Within each slab, four left columns are the period's source classes; four right columns are the destination classes. Particle streams flow between them, colored by class. Press play to advance through the periods one at a time, or scroll back to see all six together.
